The Art Of Jewellery

As members of the Association for Contemporary Jewellery We believe that jewellery does not have to be traditional, it can be an artwork in its own right. We have scoured the UK to provide a curated collection of contemporary handmade jewellery all available to purchase at the click of a button. Whether you are interested in big statement pieces, acrylic jewellery or unusual silver jewellery we have something for everyone. Browse our jewellery now to buy a beautifully crafted piece for yourself or a gift of jewellery for an artist in your life.

Modern Jewellery Designers

Our focus is on great innovative jewellery design. We collaborate with exciting up-and-coming designers like acrylic jewellery designer Rachel Clark already featured in Jewellery Focus and established designers such as Meredith Chambers who has designed jewellery collections for the Tate. Browse the list of our featured independent UK jewellery designers to find out more about them and the work they have created.

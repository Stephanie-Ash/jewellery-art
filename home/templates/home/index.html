The Art Of Jewellery

We believe that jewellery does not have to be traditional, it can be an artwork in its own right. Beautiful original jewellery pieces are not always easy to find so we have scoured the UK to provide a curated collection of contemporary handmade jewellery all available to purchase at the click of a button. Whether you are interested in big statement pieces, acrylic jewellery or unusual silver jewellery we have something for everyone. Browse our jewellery now to buy a beautifully crafted piece for yourself or a gift of jewellery for an artist in your life.

Contemporary Jewellery Designers

Our focus is on great innovative jewellery design and this would not be possible without a close collaboration with the talented creatives behind our range. We work with exciting up-and-coming designers like acrylic jewellery designer Rachel Clark already featured in Jewellery Focus and established designers such as Meredith Chambers who has designed jewellery collections for the Tate browse the list of our featured independent UK jewellery designers to find out more about them and the work they have created.
